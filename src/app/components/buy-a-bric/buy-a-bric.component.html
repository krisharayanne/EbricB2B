Buy A BRIC
Buy A BRIC is a series of products engineered by EBRIC. It uses Avalanche blockchain
to issue digital asset tokens (BRICs) that represent ownership of real-world assets.
This blockchain-based token issuance enables fractional ownership, enabling both-
and individual investors to own fractions of assets all around the globe.
At present, Buy A BRIC consists of BRIC Estate and BRIC Energy.




BRIC Estate




A new, easier way to invest in real estate. Using the concept of fractional
ownership, EBRIC empowers investors to invest and transact in ‘fractional’
pieces of a property at their fingertips. BRIC owners can benefit from rental
yield and capital returns, without the hassle of managing these properties
and handling the tenants.
